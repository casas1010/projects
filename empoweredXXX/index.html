About Us

At Adlebridge Consulting, we strive to improve business effincy by enhancing they way our clients interact with theirs.... lives of others through universal access to fully-functional prosthetic limbs. Empowered Prosthetics leverages student brainpower and the guidance of Ph.D. candidates, professors, and business professionals to engineer and build prosthetics that utilize some of today’s premier technology.

Mission Statement

EP is a company founded by students with the belief that prosthetics should be easily accessible for all. Our goal is to alleviate the financial burden associated with buying prosthetics so that children, veterans, and the general population will be able to attain them for free or at reduced costs.

An EMG sensor allows the amputee to operate the prosthetic by picking up electrical activity produced by their muscles. This electrical activity is converted into signals that are analyzed by the processing unit, which then converts the signals into a message that is readable by the motor within the prosthetic.
