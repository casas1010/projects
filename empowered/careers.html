Available Positions

Mechanical Engineering Intern

  1. Assist with the design of our prosthetics using 3D modeling software.
  2. You will be placed on one of our design teams based off of your interests (Hand, Forearm, or Wrist).

Electrical Engineering Intern

  1. Work with Arduino and develop code to add functionality to our prosthetics.
  2. Confirm components' capabilities by designing testing methods; testing properties.

Materials Engineering Intern

  1. Assist with the development of new 3D printing filaments.
  2. Research and implement new materials to improve our products.

Industrial Engineering Intern

  1. Working with the business team to strategize long term goals.
  2. Act as an internal consultant to aid in the optimization of the entire organization.
  3. Work along side the COO to develop clever solutions to operations problems.

Artificial Intelligence/Data Science Intern

  1. Assist in the implementation of AI and Machine Learning within our products.
  2. Consult administration on the potential of upcoming technologies.
  3. Use ML techniques to provide the organization with valuable insights.

Brand Identity Intern

  1. You will have a specific role within the Brand Identity team (Website Design, Merchandise, Social Media, and Research).
  2. Use your creative potential to improve our brand image and aesthetic appeal.

Business Intern

  1. Work with the business team to strategize long term goals.
  2. Assist in formulation of solutions to logistical issues.

Human Resource Intern

  1. Assist in the recruiting of new employees.
  2. Devise solutions to employee issues within the organization.
